WHO I AM


I have a background in Film & TV Production. I am someone who is constantly pursuing extracurriculars to widen my breadth of knowledge, and while working, I decided that I wanted to create a website to showcase my paintings and make it more accessible to potential clients. I was eager to tackle the challenge of building an interface in which I had no previous experience with, and began exploring Lynda, an amazing resource with a plethora of courses on coding. As I built my personal website through the guidance of the lectures, learning about JavaScript, HTML and CSS, it became apparent that this was more of a passion than an extracurricular. I decided to take the leap into the coding industry, and went on to sharpen my skills in React / Redux, Ruby, and JavaScript.

JUST EAT

Built with Ruby on Rails (PostgreSQL), React JS, and Redux

Just Eat is a Full-Stack web application that allows users to search, discover and review restaurants. I formulated a dynamic single-page ReactJS app allowing for seamless navigation. I implemented persistent storage of users, reservations, and reviews through PostgreSQL db. Just Eat has a RESTful API through a Ruby on Rails backend, and utilizes backend logic and AJAX requests for handling search queries to generate unique results.

Galaga is an interactive game built with HTML canvas using Object-Oriented Design. I generated algorithms grounded in physics for character collision, and threaded keyboard event listeners respond to player movement. I adhered to standards of OOP patterns, enabling abstraction of functionality into reusable classes.
